About the Internship 
We are looking for analytical and business-focused BI Analyst Interns who are passionate about 
transforming data into meaningful business insights and strategic decisions. 
This internship offers hands-on exposure to business analytics, dashboard development, reporting 
systems, KPI tracking, data visualization, and AI-driven business intelligence solutions. 
Interns will work closely with Product, Growth, Finance, Operations, and Analytics teams on live 
business reporting and decision-making initiatives. 
 
Key Responsibilities 
• Collect, analyze, and interpret business data from multiple sources. 
• Assist in creating dashboards, reports, and KPI tracking systems. 
• Work on business performance analysis and trend identification. 
• Support data visualization and reporting automation activities. 
• Prepare analytical summaries and business insight reports. 
• Assist in data cleaning, validation, and quality monitoring. 
• Collaborate with cross-functional teams for business reporting requirements. 
• Support forecasting and data-driven decision-making activities.
